The objective of this session was to strengthen SQL query writing skills by practicing advanced filtering, grouping, sorting, logical operations, and pattern matching techniques. The session focused on solving multiple real-world SQL problems using different SQL clauses and operators.

## Sample Queries Practiced

### SELECT Queries
- Display all employee details
- Display employee names and salaries
- Display employees from specific departments

### WHERE Clause Queries
- Employees with salary greater than 70000
- Employees working in Hyderabad
- Employees with less than 4 years of experience

### GROUP BY & HAVING Queries
- Department-wise total salary
- Average salary by department
- Departments with more than 3 employees

### DISTINCT Queries
- Distinct departments
- Distinct cities
- Distinct experience values

### Logical & Comparison Operators
- Employees from IT department with salary above 70000
- Employees with experience greater than 5
- Employees not from Sales department

### LIKE Operator Queries
- Employee names starting with 'R'
- Employee names ending with 'a'
- Departments ending with 's'
